Involvement & Activities

There are many different clubs, social events, and community service projects people can get involved in around campus. There is an improv club, greek life, club sports and weekly service for example. I have dabbled in a couple of different clubs of my own. This page goes into further detail about what each is about!

Greek Life around SJU: Sigma Sigma Sigma

I am a member of Tri Sigma at Saint Joseph's University (SJU.) Tri Sigma was founded on April 20th, 1898 at Longwood College. It was the first sorrority at SJU and was chartered in 1988. Our Philanthropy is the Robbie Page Memorial Fund. If you would like to see more about our chapter, we have an instagram, a twitter, a tumblr and a local website that I have attached below!

Big Brothers Big Sisters of America

This organization (in my case) sends college students to different elementary/middle schools and becomes sort of like their mentor or big brother / sister. This program builds up the commmunity while also helping children set and strive to achieve their goal both academically and socially by assisting them in the right directions. Intramural Volleyball

This was just a fun extra activity to play with my friends around campus. We had our team then we would play other students in our SJU community. Surprisingly, things got very competitive and it is a great way to be involved physically, but not have the time commitment or skill as clubs.
